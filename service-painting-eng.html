Car painting

МWe carry out painting of cars of all models, regardless of age, technical condition and nature of damage.

Our company employs a high quality of company spectral materials and ceramic tiles that allow to achieve the best effect and standard of paint repair.

ЭThe stages of painting include:

  • - cleaning surfaces, removing them from dust and dirt and an oily residue;
  • - plaster and levelling, abrasive compositions;
  • - primer (several layers), computer selection of paint;
  • - complete painting of a car body, followed by drying, and finishing (grinding, polishing).

We guarantee full convergence of shades and colors.

High quality services for the painting of the car body is achieved through:

  • - the use of modern equipment, the latest tools technologies and certified consumables;
  • - a wide range of additional services and professional consultations, recommendations for applying paint and lacquer coating with the features of damage and the specific brand of car.

Full car painting is the best way to change the appearance of the vehicle or restore it to its original condition and value after long operation. It's also necessary if you want to sell a car.

The staff of ZS-SERVICE will perform a full painting of the car quickly and efficiently. The process removes small defects, restores the integrity of the coating of the body. A proper fully fledged painting of a car is warranted to be free of scratches and cracks, protection against defects and driving pleasure!
