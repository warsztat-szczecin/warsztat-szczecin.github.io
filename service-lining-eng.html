Auto straightening

The ZS-SERVICE offers to its customers body straightening repair with the use of modern materials and technologies.

Types of damage our specialists work with:

  • - symmetry of the hood;
  • - swarp front spars;
  • - symmetry of the tailgate opening;
  • - swarp of the rear side members;
  • - symmetry of doorways;
  • - skewed glass opening windshield or rear;
  • - deformation of the pillars;
  • - deformation of removable and non-removable elements.

A body repair performed at a high professional level is a guarantee of a perfect appearance of the car, reliable protection of machine parts and peace of mind of the car owner.

ZS-SERVICE has a modern equipment that allows specialists to carry out body repairs of any complexity, regardless of the size of the damaged areas of the car body. We will complete all work as soon as possible.
