Retro cars restoration

The ZS-SERVICE company makes restoration of retro cars at the highest level. Our experts have many years of working experience with an exclusive cars and can provide any services related to the operation of vintage cars: from diagnostics to comprehensive world-class restoration with complete disassembly and assembly of cars.

The master uses the full technological cycle for factory technology in the work.

We are restoring original parts in need of repair to a condition consistent with the new. If the item cannot be repaired, we purchase a new original or new one.

Following stages are:

  • - dismantling a retro car, cataloging parts, removing old paint, cleaning the body;
  • - repair (manufacturing) of body elements;
  • - restoration of the car chassis;
  • - overhaul of the engine and gearbox;
  • - galvanic work in the restoration of car parts;
  • - painting work;
  • - restoration of the car;
  • - manufacture and repair of electrical wiring;
  • - car assembly and testing.
