Restoration refinishing

Quality paintwork restoration is the basic concept in the restoration of the car. Defects and damage to the coating machines can occur not only in traffic accidents, but also appear as the operation of the vehicle. Numerous small chips from stones, scrapes is not only degrade the appearance of the car but can also cause corrosion which forces you to replace body parts with their subsequent painting. The modern technology that we use in the ZS-SERVICE allows to restoring the paint of your car without removing the elements while the quality of work is at the highest level.

The methods that we use:

  • - local colouring is the simplest recovery method is applicable in case if the damage to the paintwork is restricted to a small area and not confined to a single body parts, e.g., bumpers. Fix deep scratches, scuffs and chips carried out in several stages: preparatory, main and final;
  • - spot painting is applied in case if size of damage is limited. As a rule, this method allows to resolve small defects, chips that can not be eliminated by polishing;
  • - restorative polishing body is the most common method of restoring the paintwork, gives the opportunity for just 1 procedure to resolve a wide range of defects, including: mechanical, chemical, physical damage.

Contact our professional craftsmen, who use quality equipment and supplies, you are guaranteed to obtain the complete elimination of damage with preservation of the factory paint.
